Standardized Tests

Applying to most graduate programs requires you to take a standardized test called the Graduate Record Exam (GRE). The GRE General Test measures your ability to answer basic math and English questions, which is something that most graduate programs require.


In addition to the GRE, you need to take the TOEFL (or the IELTS) exam if you belong to a country (like India) where English is not the official language. TOEFL is an English proficiency test to ensure you can adequately read, write, speak and understand the English language.


Your program or university may also require you to take the GRE Subject Test for your respective subject, however, this is quite rare.


It is important to score well on these tests in order to ensure that your profile remains competitive. A low score will drastically reduce your chances of getting into your dream university.
